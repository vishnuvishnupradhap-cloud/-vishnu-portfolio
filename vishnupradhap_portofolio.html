01 — Profile

Trained to notice what's out of place.

I'm an M.Sc. Data Science student who spends most of my time turning messy, real-world data — UPI transaction logs, legal contracts, camera footage — into models that make a defensible call. My work sits at the intersection of classical machine learning and the BI layer that explains it to the people who actually make decisions: Scikit-learn on one side, Power BI and DAX on the other.

I'm looking to bring that same instinct for pattern and anomaly to automation problems at Devopstrio — building pipelines that don't just run, but flag the moment something stops looking normal.
